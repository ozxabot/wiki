A piece of block used for terrain or zappable ores. Acts like a [[Pixel]], but textured.

The material slider controls the type of material or ore. The following types exist:
- Grass
- Dirt
- Stone
- Hard Stone/Granite
- Diamond Ore
- Ruby Ore
- Emerald Ore
- Topaz Ore
- Copper Ore
- Iron Ore
- Aluminium Ore
- Aluminium Ore (Duplicate)
