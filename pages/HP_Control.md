Controls the HP of a robot.

The robots current HP can be read from **`OUT0`**. To set the HP of a robot, set **`IN0`** to 1.0 and **`IN1`** to the desired HP percentage.

## Socket information
- **`IN0:`** Activate
- **`IN1:`** New HP
- **`OUT0:`** Current HP
